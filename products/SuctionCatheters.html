Suction Catheters®

Product Overview

Product Name: Suction Catheters®
Generic: ENDO BRONCHIAL SUCTION CATHETER
Product Code: GS-2006 / GS-2030 / GS-2030TS / GS-2022
Product Category: Anesthesia

Features

Suction Catheter (GS-2006 / GS-2030 / GS-2030TS / GS-2022)

Suitable for removal of secretions from mouth, trachea and bronchial tubes.
Manufactured from non-toxic, non-irritant medical grade PVC.
Distal end is open with one lateral eye.
Frozen surface catheter tubing for smooth intubation.
Sterile, Peelable soft blister.

Available Models

Model Feature
Suction Catheter® Plain (GS-2006) Provided with universal funnel shaped connector for safe connection to the standard suction equipment.
Suction Catheter® TC (GS-2030) Provided with "T" type vacuum control valve with thumb control for proper manoeuvring.
Suction Catheter ® TS (GS-2030TS) Distal open tip with two Lateral eyes.
Suction Catheter® FT (GS-2022) Provided with "Y" shape vacuum control valve with finger tip control facility.
